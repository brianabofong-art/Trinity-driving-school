Our Story

Built on
Experience &
Road Safety

Founded in 2015, Trinity Driving School was established with one clear mission: to produce safe, confident, and responsible drivers for the roads of Cameroon.

Our CEO, Muluh Shadrack, is a retired Regional Delegate of Transport in Cameroon and a retired Inspector of Services at the Ministry of Transport in Yaoundé — with over 22 years of experience in road safety activities and training. He is also a part-time university lecturer in Transport and Logistics.

His deep expertise guides every aspect of our teaching — from theory classes to on-road practice. We train drivers in categories B, C, D, E, and G.
